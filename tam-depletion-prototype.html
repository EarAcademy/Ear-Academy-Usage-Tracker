How Metrics Are Calculated

  • % Contacted: (All contacted schools / TAM) × 100 | ⚠️ Warning at 80%
  • Hard NO Rate: (Schools who said NO / Total contacted) × 100 | 🚨 Critical at 60%
  • Convertible Pool: Uncontacted + Replied + In Pipeline (excludes Hard NOs and Customers)
  • National Runway: Can we hit the 494-school target with what's left?
    • Convertible Pool = All schools we haven't lost or won yet
    • Expected NOs = Pool × Current Hard NO Rate (realistic projection)
    • Net Available = Pool - Expected NOs (what we can actually convert)
    • Still Need = 494 target - Current customers
    • Runway = (Net Available - Still Need) / Still Need × 100%
    • Example: +24% = We have 24% MORE opportunities than needed (comfortable margin)
    • Example: -15% = We're SHORT by 15% (can't hit target even with perfect conversion)
  • Provincial Runway: Same calculation per province
    • 🟢 >50% = Healthy runway, plenty of margin
    • 🟡 0-50% = Tight but achievable
    • 🚨 <0% = Mathematically underwater, can't hit 20% target for this province
